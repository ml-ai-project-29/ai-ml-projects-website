Frequently Asked Questions - Get Your Answers

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project completion time varies based on complexity. Simple ML projects typically take 3-5 days, while more complex applications may take 1-2 weeks. We always provide realistic timelines during the quote process.

Do you provide project documentation?

Yes! Every project comes with comprehensive documentation including detailed technical reports (15-20 pages), fully commented source code, step-by-step installation guides, and professional presentation slides.

What if I need modifications after delivery?

We offer one round of free modifications within 7 days of project delivery to ensure you're completely satisfied. Additional modifications are available at a nominal cost.

Do you work with specific datasets?

Absolutely! We can work with your provided datasets or suggest appropriate datasets for your project requirements. We also help with data preprocessing and cleaning if needed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept secure online payments through various methods including credit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers. Payment is required after project approval and before development begins.

Can you help me understand the project after delivery?

Yes! We offer optional video explanation sessions where our experts walk you through the entire project, explain the code, and answer any questions you have. This ensures you can confidently present your work.
